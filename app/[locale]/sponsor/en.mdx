## <span id="why">Why support us?</span>

The University Orchestra Polyphonia Zürich offers students and staff of ETH Zürich and the University of Zürich the opportunity to work on and perform challenging symphonic works together. We are an important part of the cultural life at Zurich's universities.

### Our Costs

Producing our concerts incurs significant costs:
- Venue rentals for rehearsals and concerts
- Sheet music and performance rights
- Technical equipment and infrastructure
- Public relations and marketing
- Organization and administration

### Your Support Makes a Difference

With your help, we can:
- Continuously improve the quality of our concerts
- Create attractive programs featuring both well-known and lesser-known works
- Promote and develop our members musically
- Enrich the cultural offerings at the universities
- Guarantee affordable ticket prices for our audience

In addition to financial contributions, we also welcome in-kind donations such as:
- Instruments and accessories
- Sheet music

- Catering for concert events

## <span id="benefits">Your Benefits as a Sponsor</span>

Depending on the level of your support, we offer various benefits:

### Basic Sponsor (from CHF 500)
- Recognition as a sponsor on our website
- Mention in our concert programs
- Thank you letter from the board

### Bronze Sponsor (from CHF 1,000)
- All Basic Sponsor benefits
- Logo placement on our website
- Free tickets for our concerts (2 tickets per concert)

### Silver Sponsor (from CHF 2,500)
- All Bronze Sponsor benefits
- Logo in concert program
- Free tickets for our concerts (4 tickets per concert)
- Invitation to our rehearsals

### Gold Sponsor (from CHF 5,000)
- All Silver Sponsor benefits
- Prominent logo placement on all communication materials
- Free tickets for our concerts (6 tickets per concert)
- Personal support and invitation to exclusive events
- Opportunity to co-design a concert framework program
